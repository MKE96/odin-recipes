Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with coffee, mascarpone and lady fingers.

Ingredients

  • 3 tsp instant coffee granules
  • 3 tbsp coffee liqueur
  • 250g tub mascarpone
  • 85g condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4-6 sponge fingers
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Mix the coffee granules with 2 tbsp boiling water in a large jug and stir to combine.
  2. Add the coffee liqueur and 75ml cold water and pour into a shallow dish and set aside.
  3. Make the cream layer by beating the mascarpone, condensed milk and vanilla extract with an electric whisk until thick and smooth.
  4. Break the sponge fingers into two or three pieces and soak in the coffee mixture for a few seconds.
  5. Put a few bits of the sponge in the bottom of two wine or sundae glasses and top with the cream.
  6. Sift over the cocoa and chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour before serving.
